WIZKID

GRAMMY AWARD WINNING AFROBEATS SINGER AND SONGWRITTER

Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun ( born 16th july 1990), known professionally as Wizkid is a Nigerian singer and songwriter. A prominent figure in the modern day Afrobeats music scene, Wizkid is regarded as one of the biggest and most influential African artists of all time. He began recording music at the age of 11 and released a collaborative album with the glorious five(5) a group he and a couple of his church friends formed. In 2009, Wizkid signed a record deal with Banky Ws Empire Mates Entertainment(E.M.E). He later rose to the limelight after releasing "holla at your boy", the lead single from his debut album, Superstar (2011), which also spawned the singles "Tease me/Bad guys" and "dont dull".
